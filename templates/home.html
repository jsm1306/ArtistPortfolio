SEEMA KOHLI


Born in 1960, Seema Kohli has created her own niche in the world of contemporary art since past 35 years. Her creative repertoire is eclectic, encompassing a wide range of mediums ranging from painting, murals, experiential installation performances, films to installations, sculptures and each a unique expression of her style.

She has over time brought a synergy of her unique sensibilities in her art. Kohli's works reveal a claiming of feminine subjectivity and an altered concept of feminine sexuality.
